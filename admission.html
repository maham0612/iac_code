Admissions at IAC Lahore

IAC welcomes applications from all Pakistani and international students, regardless of colour, creed, gender or age, encouraging diversity in terms of education, ethnicity, and socio-economic status. This includes underprivileged, minority, and disabled candidates.

Fall 2024 Admissions are open now for undergraduate programs. Sep 15, 2024 is the last date to apply for admissions.

Eligibility Requirements

Undergraduate applicants need a minimum of 45% in Intermediate (Second Division) For GCE O/A Levels, American High School Diploma, or International Baccalaureate, an IBCC equivalence certificate is necessary Applicants awaiting results may apply but must submit final results and equivalence certificates promptly.

Admission Procedure

Get started on the creative and most productive journey of your academic life at IAC! Follow the steps below to complete your registration.

  • Applicants must deposit the Admission Processing Fee (PKR 1,000/-) with the designated bank .
  • The applicant must print the challan form, fill it out, and go to the nearest branch of Faisal Bank for submission of the processing fee.
  • Applicants who have submitted the Admission Processing Fee shall be sent their Roll Number Slips via email for the admission test.
  • He/she must keep a copy of the bank challan for admission if selected.

Note:Bring original documents and attested copies for verification at admission time.
