IAC Leadership

At the Institute for Art & Culture (IAC), our leadership team is the driving force behind our mission to cultivate creativity and innovation. Since our inception in 2018, our leaders have been committed to fostering an environment where students can thrive, both academically and personally.

Our leadership comprises experienced educators, industry experts, and visionary thinkers who are dedicated to ensuring that IAC remains at the forefront of art and cultural education. They bring a wealth of knowledge and a passion for excellence that permeates every aspect of our institution.

Under their guidance, IAC has developed a state-of-the-art 18-acre green campus equipped with advanced studios, amphitheaters, a job placement center, and a Center for Well-Being and Positive Psychology. Their strategic vision ensures that our programs are not only comprehensive.

The IAC leadership team is committed to bridging the gap between classroom learning and real-world application. By emphasizing market-aligned education, training, and research, they prepare our students to meet and exceed industry demands. Our leaders inspire students to innovate, think critically, and tackle future challenges with confidence and creativity.

MR. MUHAMMAD FAISAL JANJUA

Chancellor

Institute for Art and Culture (IAC)

IAC provides a dynamic international environment, top-notch research, and an interdisciplinary education that blends campus and hands-on learning. We nurture graduates through a collaborative platform, fostering creativity and instilling problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

In response to societal challenges, we focus on arts education, emphasizing cultural diversity and interdisciplinary research. Soft skills training is integral, covering careful listening, critical questioning, logical thinking, clear communication, creative action, and ethical living.

Alongside academic growth in science and technology, student-driven extracurricular initiatives create an organic learning environment, meeting local and global market needs. Our aim is to offer students access to a collaborative academic setting with international partners and diverse programs.
